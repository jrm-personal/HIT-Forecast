Course shelter — wind direction guide

The slalom course sits in the channel east of Heirisson Island, downstream of the Causeway Bridge. Southeast and East winds are the best — they come over Charles Paterson Park, Burswood and the eastern bank, which break up the fetch before it reaches the course. West, South and Northwest are average — Heirisson Island, the southern bank, or the angled fetch keep things workable but not glassy. North and Northeast are the worst — open Perth Water fetch flattens straight onto the course. Southwest is also bad.
